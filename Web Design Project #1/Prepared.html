Restocking on Supplies


Living during the Covid pandemic has made almost everyone paranoid and germaphobic. When I went back down to the city for winter break, I wasn't worried since my mom stocked up on Lysol dienfectant products as soon as Covid cases were being reported. She made sure to get masks, disenfectant wipes and sprays and hand sanitizers.
