Carribean Resturant Outing: Dessert


This is banana bread with a side of vanilla ice cream. This was one of the desserts at a Carribean resturant that my family and I went to about a week after I came home for winter break. The banana bread by itself was very warm and it melted in yoour mouth. Mixing it with the ice cream gave that kick of "cold freshness" with the warmth of the banana bread only enhanced the eating experience.
